EmpireForge - Rebuild. Survive. Dominate.

The world has fallen. From the ashes of civilization, factions of survivors compete to rebuild. Lead your faction through the wasteland, rediscover lost technologies, build settlements, forge alliances, and dominate the ruins of the old world.

Features:
• Build and manage settlements across the wasteland
• Rediscover 80+ lost technologies from the old world
• Lead your faction against 25+ rival survivor groups
• Multiple victory paths: Military, Scientific, Cultural, Diplomatic
• Leaderboard system - compete with players worldwide
• Achievement system with 10 milestones to unlock
• Victory Progress Dashboard - track your path to victory
• City Production Panel - monitor all settlements at a glance
• Diplomacy Quick Panel - see all faction relations instantly
• Turn Summary Preview - plan your next move
